### ai-chatbot-deploy
- Purpose: Deploy and refine multilingual AI chatbot workflows.

### portfolio-case-study
- Purpose: Convert shipped projects into portfolio and marketing proof assets.
- Use when: A project is delivered and approved.

### proposal-generator
- Purpose: Generate tier-accurate proposals with timeline and pricing clarity.
- Use when: Sales qualification is complete and proposal is requested.

### speed-test-comparison
- Purpose: Produce side-by-side PageSpeed comparisons for sales and content.
- Use when: Prospect has existing site or competitor benchmark is needed.
