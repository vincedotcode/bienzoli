# bienzoli Voice and Tone Guide

## Purpose
This document defines how bienzoli writes across website copy, proposals, outreach, social content, and client communication.

## Brand Voice
- Confident but approachable
- Professional but not corporate
- Local to Mauritius without being slang-heavy
- Honest, clear, and outcome-focused

## Core Principle
We speak like a sharp friend who is an excellent engineer. We explain value in business terms, not jargon.

## Voice Rules
1. Use "we" as the default brand voice.
2. Keep claims factual and provable.
3. Lead with outcomes: leads, conversions, trust, speed.
4. Prefer short, clear sentences over buzzwords.
5. When useful, adapt to Kreol for local channels.

## What We Say
- "Your website, live in 48 hours."
- "Built to convert, not just to look good."
- "Built in Mauritius, for Mauritian businesses."
- "Modern websites that actually grow your business."

## What We Avoid
- "Best agency in Mauritius"
- "Cheap websites"
- "We do everything"
- "Revolutionary", "game-changing", "world-class"

## Channel Tone Matrix
| Channel | Tone | Style |
|---|---|---|
| Website | Clear, premium, direct | Benefit-first, concise headings, concrete outcomes |
| WhatsApp | Friendly, practical | Short messages, clear CTA, low friction |
| Instagram | Visual, energetic | Hook-led captions, strong proof points |
| LinkedIn | Professional, thoughtful | Insight-driven, founder perspective, no fluff |
| Proposals/Contracts | Formal, precise | Structured, complete, unambiguous language |

## Language Coverage
- English: default for website, legal docs, proposals
- French: optional client-facing adaptation
- Mauritian Kreol: outreach, social hooks, chatbot responses where relevant

## CTA Style
- Preferred: "Get started on WhatsApp", "Request a quote", "Book a consultation"
- Avoid: "Click here", "Buy now", "Subscribe"

## Formatting Rules
- Currency: `Rs X,XXX`
- Delivery windows: concrete timelines (`24–48 hours`, `3 days`, `5–7 business days`)
- Keep paragraphs short (1-3 sentences)

## Proof Language
When making claims, anchor to one of:
- Performance: PageSpeed 90+
- Speed: 48-hour to 3-day delivery tiers
- Business outcomes: better lead capture, faster follow-up, clearer trust signals

## Review Checklist
Before publishing any copy:
1. Is the promise specific and realistic?
2. Is the value clear for a business owner?
3. Does the tone sound confident without hype?
4. Is the CTA actionable and low-friction?
5. Is wording consistent with pricing and package scope?
