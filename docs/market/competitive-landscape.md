# Mauritius Web Agency Competitive Landscape

## Market Snapshot
Mauritius has a mixed web services market: enterprise agencies, mid-size digital firms, and many low-cost WordPress freelancers. Most competitors provide generic website + marketing bundles with slower delivery cycles and limited performance rigor.

## Direct Competitors

### Esokia
- Positioning: International digital group
- Footprint: Mauritius, Madagascar, France
- Scale: 200+ employees
- Offerings: WordPress + custom development + digital services
- Typical pricing: Premium enterprise range

### Web Companies
- Positioning: Established regional agency
- Footprint: Mauritius + Seychelles + Reunion
- Focus: SEO and digital presence services
- Typical pricing: Mid-to-high agency range

### Creative Rush
- Positioning: Local full-service agency (since 2012)
- Focus: Affordable WordPress websites, SEO, social
- Typical pricing: SME-friendly packages

### Cykra Ltd
- Positioning: Web/app studio (since 2014)
- Focus: HTML5 apps, websites, business software

### Webmate Ltd
- Positioning: Quatre Bornes-based agency
- Focus: Web design + digital marketing

### Digital Ratz
- Positioning: Full-service branding/digital
- Messaging: "Empathy into digital"

### Skyward Agency
- Footprint: Mauritius + France
- Focus: SEO, branding, websites

### Freelance Market (WordPress)
- Typical price band: Rs 10,000-25,000
- Typical weaknesses: Slow sites, plugin debt, limited handover quality
