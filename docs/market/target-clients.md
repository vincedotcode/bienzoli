# bienzoli Target Clients by Tier

## FLIC EN FLAC (Rs 8,000)
- Persona: Solo operator launching a first website
- Examples: Mobile hairdresser, food vendor, freelance tutor, market stall, handyman
- Discovery channels: Facebook Marketplace, Instagram, neighborhood WhatsApp groups, word-of-mouth
- Typical objection: "I don't need a website, I use Facebook"
- Close trigger: "Businesses with websites get found on Google and look more trustworthy"
- Sales angle: Fast launch + affordable entry + WhatsApp lead capture

## PORT LOUIS (Rs 18,000)
- Persona: Small business owner ready for a professional web presence
- Examples: Restaurants, salons, studios, small retail, tutors with teams
- Discovery channels: Google searches, referral network, IG profiles with Linktree-only funnels
- Typical objection: "Rs 18,000 is expensive"
- Close trigger: Monthly option (Rs 1,800/mo) + lead conversion value
- Sales angle: Professional credibility + better lead follow-up + low monthly entry

## GRAND BAIE (Rs 35,000)
- Persona: Growing business needing automation and stronger trust signals
- Examples: Clinics, real estate agents, tour operators, law/accounting firms
- Discovery channels: LinkedIn, referrals, networking groups, industry events
- Typical objection: "Can you do it for less?"
- Close trigger: Time saved by AI chatbot + lead handling automation
- Sales angle: Operational efficiency + premium positioning + analytics clarity

## LE MORNE (Rs 60,000+)
- Persona: Established operator needing a custom platform
- Examples: Hotel groups, rentals, franchises, SaaS startups, ecommerce brands
- Discovery channels: Direct outreach, LinkedIn, tourism/business networks, strategic referrals
- Typical objection: "We already work with a big agency"
- Close trigger: Delivery speed + practical AI integration + tighter iteration cycles
- Sales angle: Custom system ownership + faster implementation + measurable ROI

## Qualification Heuristics
1. Urgency: Is there a concrete timeline?
2. Business impact: Is there a clear conversion or operations goal?
3. Data readiness: Do they have enough content/assets to move fast?
4. Budget fit: Which tier matches needs without over-selling?
5. Upsell path: Is there a natural retainer or higher-tier progression?
