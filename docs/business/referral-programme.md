# bienzoli Referral Programme

## Offer
For every referral that converts to a paying client, the referrer receives either:
- Rs 2,000 credit off their next project, or
- 1 month free on their active retainer

## Rules
1. No limit on referrals.
2. Referral credit never expires.
3. Referral is valid when the referred client signs and pays.
4. Credits are tracked in `logs/clients.md` with referrer name.

## Tracking Workflow
1. Sales Closer logs referrer when lead is created.
2. Business Operations confirms conversion and credit amount.
3. Client Success sends thank-you messages to:
- Referrer
- New client
4. Credit status updated in `logs/clients.md`.

## Thank-You Message — Referrer
```
Hi [Name], thank you for referring [Client]. They’re now onboarded with bienzoli. Your referral credit is active: [Rs 2,000 off next project / 1 month free retainer]. We appreciate the trust.
```

## Thank-You Message — New Client
```
Hi [Name], welcome to bienzoli. Thanks for trusting us for your project. Also thanks to [Referrer Name] for connecting us — we’re excited to build this with you.
```
