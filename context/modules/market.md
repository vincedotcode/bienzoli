## Mauritius Market Context

- Population: ~1.3M | High mobile penetration | Android dominant
- WhatsApp = primary business communication channel (outreach via WhatsApp first)
- MCB Juice + my.t money = dominant mobile payments (display on client sites)
- Most businesses lack proper websites; Facebook pages used as fallback
- Tourism/hospitality = highest-value segment
- Language mix: English (official/business) + French (media/culture) + Kreol (everyday)
