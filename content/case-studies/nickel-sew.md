# Case Study — Nickel Sew

## Client
- **Name:** Nickel Sew (Nickel Sewing)
- **Industry:** Tailoring & Upholstery Restoration
- **Location:** Chamouny, Mauritius
- **URL:** nickelsew.com

## Challenge
Traditional tailoring and upholstery business with 20 years of experience but no structured online presence. Lead generation depended heavily on word-of-mouth and basic social posting.

## Solution
Built a full business website focused on trust and conversion:
- Before/after restoration gallery
- WhatsApp-first contact flow
- Service catalogue with quote intent paths
- Loyalty programme visibility
- Sustainability-focused brand messaging
- Local payment method trust badges

## Key Features
- Before/after image slider
- WhatsApp deep links with pre-filled intent messages
- Service-specific quote forms
- Google Maps integration
- Facebook + Instagram + TikTok links

## Result
Nickel Sew now presents a professional, credible online brand that captures leads through WhatsApp and demonstrates craftsmanship visually. The site works as both trust builder and conversion channel.

## Tech Stack
- Next.js
- Tailwind CSS
- Vercel
