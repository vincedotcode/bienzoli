## The Challenge

DS Nails was a home-based nail salon growing through Instagram — beautiful nail art posts, a growing following, and genuine demand. But the entire customer journey was a friction loop: see a post → comment or DM → wait for a reply → discuss availability → eventually confirm.

The core problem: **Instagram is a great discovery channel but a terrible booking system.** Every new enquiry required the same back-and-forth conversation. DS Nails was spending 2–3 hours a day answering the same questions: "What services do you offer? How much is a gel set? Are you available Saturday?"

The secondary problem: **Instagram discovery has a quality ceiling.** Potential new clients looking for nail salons in Quatre Bornes couldn't find DS Nails via Google. The business existed only for people who were already following them or found them by hashtag browsing.

---

## The Solution

bienzoli built a dedicated salon website that turned DS Nails' Instagram aesthetics into a conversion-optimized booking platform — with a full service catalogue, gallery, testimonials, and authenticated booking capability.

The service catalogue was the most important element. Pricing, duration, and a photo example for every service meant that visitors arrived at the booking flow already knowing exactly what they wanted and what it cost. The "which service should I book?" question gets answered by the website, not in a DM.

The booking system was built with authentication so DS Nails could manage appointment requests structured, rather than through informal WhatsApp threads. Clients create an account, request an appointment, and receive confirmation — a professional experience that elevates the perception of the business.

---

## Features Built

- **Service cards with price and duration** — every service listed with: service name, clear price, estimated duration, and a sample photo. No "DM for pricing" ambiguity.
- **Nail art gallery** — portfolio grid showcasing the best work, organized by style (gel, acrylic, nail art). Acts as both proof and inspiration.
- **Client testimonials** — 5 verified client reviews with real quotes. Social proof for potential first-time clients who haven't been referred.
- **Authentication-enabled booking flow** — client account creation, appointment request form (service, date, time preference, notes), request submission.
- **Instagram social proof integration** — follower count and latest posts pulled from Instagram profile.
- **WhatsApp floating button** — persistent for clients who prefer direct contact over booking form.
- **About section** — personal story of DS Nails, building emotional connection and trust with potential clients.
- **Mobile-optimized throughout** — nail salon clients primarily browse on phone. Every interaction tested at 375px.

## What Made This Project Distinctive

The service pricing transparency was the key design decision. Many salons in Mauritius use "DM for pricing" to avoid price comparison or to leave room for negotiation. bienzoli's recommendation to DS Nails: publish every price. Transparency converts faster than mystery. Clients who know the price before they enquire are more qualified leads — they've already decided they can afford it.

The authenticated booking system elevated DS Nails from "home-based nail artist" to "professional nail studio with a booking platform." The perception shift is significant — and in the beauty industry, perception is directly tied to pricing power. A nail artist with a booking system can charge more than one who takes appointments through Instagram DMs.
