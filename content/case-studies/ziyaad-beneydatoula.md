# Case Study — Ziyaad Ben Eydatoula

## Client
- **Name:** Ziyaad Ben Eydatoula
- **Industry:** Product Management Consulting
- **Location:** London, UK
- **URL:** ziyaadbeneydatoula.com

## Challenge
A senior product consultant with enterprise experience needed a premium digital presence that positioned him as a strategic advisor, not a generic freelancer.

## Solution
Built a consultancy website with high-trust narrative and clear conversion paths:
- Problem-framing sections ("Sound familiar?")
- Four service offerings with tabbed detail
- Testimonial carousel
- Company logo credibility section
- CV download and contact flow

## Key Features
- Interactive problem cards
- Service detail tabs
- Real-quote testimonial carousel
- Company logo showcase
- Downloadable CV
- "Touch base" contact flow

## Result
The website positions the consultant at a premium level, communicates enterprise credibility quickly, and improves lead qualification through problem-aware copy structure.

## Tech Stack
- Next.js
- Tailwind CSS
- Vercel
