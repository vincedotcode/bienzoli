## The Challenge

Ziyaad Ben Eydatoula is a senior product consultant with 12+ years of enterprise experience at SEGA, Teradata, and Tesco. His credibility was real — but invisible to the market. He had a LinkedIn profile and word-of-mouth referrals. No personal brand website. No conversion-optimized online presence.

The core challenge: **at the premium consulting level, your website is your first credibility filter.** A potential client from a Fortune 500 company will check your website before taking a call. If there's nothing there — or something generic — the conversation starts at a disadvantage.

The secondary challenge: **consultants are not products.** You can't list a price and a "buy now" button. The website needed to do something subtler: build resonance with the exact problems the ideal client is experiencing, demonstrate category expertise, and create a clear path to a conversation — not a transaction.

---

## The Solution

bienzoli built a consultancy-grade personal brand website that positions Ziyaad as a strategic advisor, not a hired implementer. The key insight: **the best consulting sites don't lead with the consultant — they lead with the client's problems.**

The site opens with a "Sound familiar?" section listing the exact friction points that Ziyaad's ideal clients experience: misaligned product roadmaps, stakeholder conflict, slow delivery despite large teams. Before the visitor reads a single word about Ziyaad's credentials, they've nodded to three or four problems they recognize. The website is talking about them, not about him.

From that foundation, the service offerings and credentials serve as the answer — not the pitch. The structure creates a natural progression: "You understand my problem → you've solved it before → I want to talk to you."

---

## Features Built

- **Problem-framing "Sound familiar?" section** — 6 interactive problem cards covering the most common enterprise product management pain points. Hover states reveal brief descriptions. This section qualifies the visitor before they read credentials.
- **Four service offerings with tabbed detail** — Product Strategy, Team Effectiveness, Roadmap Clarity, Stakeholder Alignment. Each tab expands with scope, what's included, and a typical engagement context.
- **Real-quote testimonial carousel** — 4 testimonials from named enterprise contacts at real companies. Carousel format keeps the section compact without truncating valuable social proof.
- **Company logo credibility section** — logos of organizations Ziyaad has worked with (SEGA, Teradata, Tesco, others). Instant enterprise credibility signal.
- **Downloadable CV** — PDF CV download available directly from the site. Useful for clients who need to share credentials with hiring committees.
- **"Touch base" contact flow** — low-friction contact section that avoids generic "Contact Us" framing. Directs to email with a suggested subject line.
- **Smooth scroll navigation** — single-page layout with anchor navigation. Fast, focused reading experience.
- **Open Graph image** — professional meta image for when the URL is shared on LinkedIn (the primary distribution channel for consultancy leads).

## What Made This Project Distinctive

This was bienzoli's first international client and first premium personal brand build. Two things made it stand out:

**Problem-aware copywriting as the lead mechanism.** Most consultant sites lead with a photo and a bio. This site leads with six problems the target client is currently experiencing. The visitor's brain responds differently to "this website understands my situation" than to "this person has impressive credentials." The conversion architecture prioritizes empathy before authority — then backs it up with authority.

**Design that serves the premium positioning.** At Rs 35,000+ rates, Ziyaad's clients are evaluating whether he belongs in the same room as their VP of Product. The site needed to signal that — through typography choices, spacing decisions, and the quality of interactions. Every hover state, every transition, every micro-interaction reinforces the premium positioning. Nothing looks like a template. Everything looks considered.

This project demonstrated that bienzoli can serve international clients and handle premium brand positioning at the same standard as agencies charging 3–4× more.
