# LinkedIn Content Guidelines

## Format
- Text posts and 16:9 images

## Content Types
- Thought leadership (lessons from real builds)
- Client success stories
- Industry insight posts
- Educational posts on web performance and conversions
- Agency-building journey updates

## Tone
- Professional, insightful, practical
- Position Vince as a young technical leader building a modern Mauritian agency

## Frequency Target
- 2 posts per week

## Post Structure
1. Problem statement
2. Practical insight
3. Real example
4. CTA or conversation starter
