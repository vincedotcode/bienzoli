# Instagram Content Guidelines

## Format
- Feed assets: 1:1
- Stories/Reels: 9:16

## Content Types
- Portfolio carousel showcases
- Build-process reels
- Testimonial quote cards
- Speed test comparisons
- Educational tips for businesses going online

## Frequency Target
- 4x/week total
- 2 feed posts + 2 stories/reels minimum

## Visual Standards
- Use bienzoli brand colors and typography.
- Keep card hierarchy clear: title, proof, CTA.
