# Outreach Asset Guidelines

## Purpose
Store and standardize outbound sales assets.

## Asset Types
- Loom/screen-recording previews
- Before/after comparison images
- Speed-test proof screenshots
- Proposal snippets

## Naming Convention
`YYYY-MM-DD-[business-name]-[asset-type].[ext]`

## Quality Rules
- Keep assets concise and specific to the prospect.
- Highlight one clear pain point and one clear improvement.
- End with a simple CTA (reply/WhatsApp/call).

## Compliance
Do not include private client data in prospect-facing assets.
